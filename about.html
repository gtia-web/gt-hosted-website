About Us

International Ambassadors at Georgia Tech (GTIA) is made up of some of the best talent Georgia Tech can offer, with members from around the world and across Tech’s disciplines.

With just over 60 tight-knit members, representing over 20 nationalities from 5 continents, we are an organization that champions international culture and diversity. We believe that in our differences there is beauty, strength and inspiration to do more.

Our talented, hardworking and honestly pretty cool members are the main reason our organization has grown in our role in the Georgia Tech community at such a great rate over the last few years. We have grown from solely a student recruitment team, to a student organization that holds multiple events for the international community through the year – from Career Fairs to Movie Nights – and caters to a community of Yellow Jackets both on campus and around the world.

Committees

Internal

The Internal Affairs Committee organizes, retreats, team hangouts, and other social events that promote building strong relationships within GTIA members. Internal also maintains the membership roster and is in charge of leading general meetings.

External

The External Affairs Committee organizes cultural events to positively impact the student community on campus. Our goal is to expose students to cultures around the world while also bringing the world to GT. Some of our previous events include Night Market, Culture Fest, Human Libraries, Language Cafe, and Cultural Showcase.

Outreach

The Outreach Committee manages campaigns to promote Georgia Tech on an international level and organize outreach efforts to reach out to prospective and admitted students around the world. Outreach is also invovled in hosting webinars that would be useful for International Students (Taxes, Interviews, Career fair, etc.).

Marketing

The Marketing Committee is in charge of producing all promotional materials and merchandising for GTIA. This covers any and all event graphics other committees may request and physical items (stickers, t-shirts, etc.).

Finance

The Finance Committee maintains records of all revenue and expenditures. Finance is responsible for hosting fundraising events, reaching out to on-campus orgs/departments for funds, and reaching out to other companies for sponsorship.
