Internship Program
Internship program is an academic program which adds work experience to formal education with practical work experience. Internships are single-semester, major related work experiences that help students better understand the work environment and the applications of their studies. These internships can be full-time or part-time and can be paid or unpaid. The global internship programs are also available.

Co-op Program
Co-op program allows students to gain at least a year's worth of experience with the same employer and earn a salary while working. Students who successfully the program receive a co-op designation on their degree and are recognized for their accomplishment during the commencement ceremony.

Career Advising
Career advice can be found at Center for Career Discovery and Development (C2D2). There are specific advisors available, and C2D2 also holds the annual career fair as well as workshops on resume building, interviewing, etc

Career Fair
Career Fair is hosted by C2D2 for companies come to Tech and talk to students that wish to apply for an internship/co-op. There is a fall and spring career fair, and many schools have their own career fairs for specific majors. Virtual career fair is also available.
